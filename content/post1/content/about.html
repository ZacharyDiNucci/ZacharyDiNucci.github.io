UNREAL ENGINE TOP DOWN SHOOTER/ Spring 2021

About this Game

Escape a secret government facility responsible for the research and creation of government warbots like yourself. Fight though the waves of bots in order to earn your freedom and escape. In this top-down shooter you will fight through 10 waves with ever increasing difficulty on 2 different levels. While the early bots might be easy their numbers can become overwhelming especially in the later levels. Be sure to continue to top off your health as the waves continue or else you might get stuck in a side room hoping the bots don’t break in.

About the Development

This project was made for a senior project of collage where I was allowed to create anything I wished and so decided to work with Unreal Engine as I wanted to learn more about the engine. Robo Beatdown was inspired by games such as SAS Zombie Assault where you must hold out as long as you can from increasingly difficult waves.

I used blender to create the 3D models and textures and felt Robots would work as they tend to look good in a low poly art style such as in this game. One aspect of the game which I am really proud of was that the player will go through 2 different levels and even though there are these I was able to keep stuff like points the same. I am also proud of the melee robot within the game because he has the first animation I ever imported from blender onto Unreal Engine.
