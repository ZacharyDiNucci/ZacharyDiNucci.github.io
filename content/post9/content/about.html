About this Game

This Application was created for my job at the GIMM development team. It is created as a touring app for downtown boise. This app uses Unity's AR foundation to work for both Apple and Andoid applications. For it I was responsable for programming the Augmented reality portions as well as helping designing parts of the app's mechanics, and while there are some bugs and some things I am going to continue working on I am happy with where it is now.

The App is split into 4 different sections each representing a different location in downtown Boise. The Basque Block Scene is the main scene and is a walk through the block telling the user about the block and the Baque people though their culture. The Jump scene is used to tell about JR Simplot and the farming origins of the boise Area. It uses a more stationary game where you do various farm chores. The last two are smaller scenes about treefort and the egyptian theater.
