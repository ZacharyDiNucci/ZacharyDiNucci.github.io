UNITY AUGMENTED REALITY APP/ Spring 2021

About this Game

Augmented Reality is a newer technology which hopefully will be able to improve the lives of those who use it. This app is to be used to help teach people how to interact in a society. It has 3 lessons using AR and 1 using an animated video. The 3 lessons are; how to ride the bus, how to keep a safe distance, and how to order food at a restaurant.

About the Development

This demo was created in collaboration with the Boise School district and was designed to help them teach students with learning disabilities skills in the community. Students would learn the skill in the classroom, such as how to order at a restaurant, then go out with a teacher and use the app to practice the skills. I developed this app with a group of about 10 people however I was the only programmer for the AR portions the rest of the people either did VR Development, Art, or Animation. For the App I used Unity AR Foundation so it could be output for either Android or IOS.

I loved working on this project because it was my first shipped product and it was great working with the teacher for the project. We would meet weekly to demonstrate progress we had made and get feedback on our progress. I ended up being a sort of leader for the AR portion and so had to contact all the artists regularly for progress updates on assets and give feedback on their creations, one example of this was a plate someone made which had over 3 million faces which is a fun story to tell.
