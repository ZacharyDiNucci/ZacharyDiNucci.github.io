Music Videos

This page is for two music videos I made in my early GIMM classes which were designed to teach how to animate using Adobe Animate. One is a mostly done video while the other is an animatic for a full video.

This Anamatic uses a song which my siblings wrote and was ment as a showcase of what I had learned with animation. I tried to tell a story of a band going on a big tour and becoming a hit with my siblings being the stars. I wanted this to be a gift for them because they were having their band break up as it was going to be hard to continue when all 4 members were in different states and I was proud of what they had done.
This Music Video was created using flash and took one year to make. The song used was High from NCS and I was inspired by the idea of history to make the video for the theme. The video is about a scientist from the future building a time machine and using it to see different times. The three he was to visit were our time, 1918, and the 1500's.
