UNITY VIRTUAL REALITY FIRST PERSON SHOOTER/ Spring 2021

About this Game

Today is the day of your school’s annual food fight. You and your classmates will battle in the cafeteria in order to see who is best. Use food to hit the other team to knock them out. Use water to clean your team and get help them last longer. In this VR FPS you are the deciding factor of who wins and losses.

About the Development

This project was originally inspired by the Valve Index controllers which were announced a few weeks before I began work on the game. I wanted to use the hand tracking of the controllers to create a wizard game based around casting spells using hand shapes. However, when a friend joked about making a food fighting game I decided to switch to that idea and it’s what led to this. I modeled and rigged the AI for this game using Blender and they were one of the first times I ever rigged an object. My brother created the soundtrack for this game which I then imported into Unity for the game.
