About Me

I grew up in Boise, Idaho and from an early age was very interested in video games. I remember my family would rent GameCubes and Xboxs from our local Hastings. One Christmas we got an Xbox of our own with Sonic Heroes, this was the first game I remember my family owning and really enjoying. Over the next few years I remember playing games on various systems such as the Nintendo DS and later the Xbox 360. Then when I was in the 7th grade I had saved enough money over the last year and a half to pay for my own computer. This was really amazing to me because it was the first system I had bought with my own money and brought me into the world of PC gaming. Later I started to get really interested in making my own games, and this lead me to learning about the GIMM major. Now that I have started down this path I am really gladd I havent looked back.

I first learned of GIMM from my mother who knew I wanted to learn how to program games and other software. Right now I have created flash games and music videos with a few VR apps coming in the future. For my future job I really want to make games for either myself or a development company. It would be a dream come true and was all started by my family going to local stores.
