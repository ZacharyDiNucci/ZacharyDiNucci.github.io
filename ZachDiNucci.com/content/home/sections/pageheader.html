Welcome

My name is Zachary DiNucci and I am a Game Designer/Programmer with a bachelor’s degree from Boise State’s College of Innovation and Design through their Games Interactive Media and Mobile Technology program. This website is to show off some of the work which I have done through normal games, multiplayer, Virtual Reality, and Augmented Reality. I find game design fascinating because I can create worlds from my imagination and have others go through them, these worlds can be inspired from history or just from going to school in the 21st century.
