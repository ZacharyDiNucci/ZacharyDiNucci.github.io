Hello!

My name is Zach DiNucci I am a junior developer, a game designer, a history buff, and a gamer. I grew up in Boise, Idaho where I attend Boise State University.I expanded my knowledge and built some of my first games through a new program at Boise State called GIMM. Along with attending classes I joined a Dungeons and Dragons club where I became a dungeon master and player. As a dungeon master I was able to fabricate worlds and stories for my players that helped me grow as a game designer.

My enjoyment and love of gaming and game designing started at a young age with the game Dinosaur 3D for the PC. My family would gather around and we would run around the island and save baby dinosaurs. Since then I have played all sorts of games from simple indie games like Unturned or Always Sometimes Monsters to big AAA games like Battlefield or Apex Legends. I have also been able to create many different themes of games and you will see those in my portfolio.

Then came Minecraft; I have hosted and modded my own servers along with creating my own special mods since I was in Junior High School around 2012. I had the opportunity to attend Minecon in 2010 in Las Vegas, Nevada where I was able to talk to game developers and listen to panels. My experience at Minecon showed me that the world of gaming technology is always growing and I became determined to become a developer in the field.

I want to continue telling stories, whether that be in my Dungeons and Dragon campaigns, stories from historical facts, or in games that I've developed. I am ready to start working with and building games with a team that also shares my love of gaming.
